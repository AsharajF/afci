Job aspirant?

Steps to avail AFCI's Placement and Training facilities

  • Step 1: Fill your details
  • Step 2: Make Payment
  • Step 3 :You will receive link to join AFCI's training and placement WhatsApp group. JOIN THE GROUP
  • Step 4: You will be directly contacted for an interview. This interview would confirm your qualifications and experience we would also analyse weather or not any extensive training if required before your placement
  • Step 4: Receive required training via offline /online mode as required
  • Step 5: Land your dream job


    • Important instructions

    A small amount of 299/- would be charged as placement fee
    You'll be a member of AFCI's placement service and this membership can be renewed every year by depositing the same amount.
    During this One year, you'll be provided with notifications and training for jobs matching your qualification and interview would be arranged by AFCI
    However, if you did not avail the opportunity provided and/or resigned from current job, you can still avail AFCI's placement service over the course of One year.

    Reason Why Choose Asharaj Foundation

    The Power Of Education.

    Good quality education has the power to change the life of a person, a community and the future of the planet. It is a fundamental human right of every woman, man and child – no matter who and where they are..

    Best Online/Offline Education.

    We provide a homely environment to both online and offline students.We know a child's mind is like wet clay, we mould the future of students in a way they'll prove to be an asset for the country.

    One For All Student.

    We provide high quality education to students coming from all classes and backgrounds with the lowest of prices understanding their financial/residential conditions.

    Our Vision

    Education and employability for all is the key to nation’s strength and prosperity. Human empowerment will lead to a strong nation.

    Our Mission

    Make education and employability within reach of all those seeking a bright career. Bridge the skill gap between academia and industry in order to ensure 100% employability

    Our Objective

    In the light of above, the ‘Asharaj Foundation’, registered under the Societies Act 1860 and managed by experts drawn from Industry, Academia, Social Sector and Business leaders came into being in order to work for addressing the employability challenges being faced by the young generation. The primary objective of the foundation is to bridge the gap between academia and industry so as to provide employable work force to Corporate Sector in addition to enhancing productivity of organizations by skill up gradation of in-service employees
