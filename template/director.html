About Director

Prof (Cdr) RP Singh, Director Asharaj Foundation, Lucknow is an alumni of Naval College of Engineering, Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT) and MDI Gurgaon. He served Indian Navy in Electrical (Avionics) branch of Engineering and was associated with INAS 300 Sea Harrier fighter planes in the capacity of HoD (ALO), Chief Engineer (SALO), Deputy Director, Joint Director and Director at various organizations of Ministry of Defence.

Prof (Cdr) RP Singh possesses 15 years of academic experience in the capacities of Deputy Director (Academics), Deputy Director (Training), Director (Training and Placement) at various Colleges/Universities such as Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT), Naval Institute of Aeronautical Technology, Integral University Lucknow, Shri Ramswaroop Memorial Group of Professional Colleges Lucknow and Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University Barabanki before taking over the present assignment as Director Asharaj Foundation, Lucknow.

The international exposure of Prof (Cdr) RP Singh includes visit to Sri Lanka, United Arab Emirate, United Kingdom, France, Germany and Israel all assignments for Technology transfer and for exposure to best global practices in engineering and education.

Prof (Cdr) RP Singh takes keen interest in providing career opportunities and employability enhancement of students for their bright future. More than 12500 students have so far bagged their placements under the mentorship of Prof (Cdr) RP Singh in addition to many in Govt jobs and defence forces.

The awards and rewards earned by Prof (Cdr) RP Singh include President’s award, Career Guru (Visionary Mind) award, Professional Excellence award and India Skill award for his exemplary work in his assigned responsibilities.

Prof (Cdr) RP Singh attended several national/international seminars and conferences on Technology, Higher education, Skill development, Career and employability, Research, quality management, Value Engineering and youth empowerment. He brings a very rich experience of value based education and career training for the benefit of the students of Asharaj Foundation, Lucknow

Director's Message

Holistic development of a nation will depend upon successful implementation of Government initiatives (Make in India, Unnat Bharat, Digital India and Atm Nirbhar Bharat etc) and availability of job ready workforce. While academia is rigid about their curriculum with lesser flexibility to change, the industry is galloping with technology up gradation and innovative business processes for higher productivity and profitability. New India has a strong urge to emerge as a fastest growing economy in the world. Asharaj Foundation has been visualized in this context in order to undertake the skill gap bridging in a dynamic manner for meeting the dual objectives of trained manpower to industry on one hand and enhancing the employability of graduating students on the other.

In order to achieve the larger objectives, the Foundation also undertakes career awareness programmes for school children and pre UG students so that the future workforce can be groomed into passionate employees in their choice domain. Career Training programmes offered by the Institute for in-service corporate employees are designed to cause efficiency multiplication, productivity enhancement, team building, increased motivation levels, re-skilling, enhanced commitment and leadership traits.

The foundation is mentored and promoted by a group of experienced and committed professionals, enlightened minds, drawn from the industry and academia. The team has taken up this social responsibility as an opportunity to give back to society their mantras of success and share the joy of creating a strong and prosperous nation.

I urge graduating students, school students, corporate employees, retiring defence personnel and other passionate youth seeking a better career to make best use of the facilities provided by Asharaj Foundation for enhancing their employability and entrepreneurial skills. The services of the Placement Cell of the Institute are made available to all students PAN India and abroad seeking guidance and support for a better career.

I also urge the recruiters and corporate houses to make use of the available talent pool of our Career Center who will be well trained, job ready and fully committed to meet the job description provided by the HR managers.

I assure one and all of our best services with total commitment and best practices.

Best wishes

Prof (Cdr) RP Singh
